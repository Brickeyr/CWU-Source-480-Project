  The Goals Of Source:

    Celebrate the depth and diversity of scholarly inquiry and creative expression at Central Washington University

    Share the accomplishments of CWU students in all disciplines and on all campuses with the community

    Recognize outstanding scholarly work and creative activities;

    Foster connections between students, faculty, alumni, and employers that help students develop as professionals in their field.
